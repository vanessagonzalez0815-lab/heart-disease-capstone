# heart-disease-capstone
Data science capstone project analyzing heart disease indicators from the BRFSS 2015 dataset from the CDC annual survey.

#Project Overview
Goal
Predict heart disease status from behavioral and socioeconomic health indicators.
Challenge
Addressing severe class imbalance to ensure models accurately detect minor disease cases.
Approach
Apply and compare machine learning models on original and binned datasets.
Outcome
Quantify socioeconomic and lifestyle inequities that contribute to increased heart disease risk across income and education groups.

#About the Data
Source (CDC): Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System Data 2015 
Data Points: 253,610 
Features: 22
Clinical Features: heart disease, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, cholesterol check, BMI, smoking status, stroke, diabetes, general health rating, mental health, physical health, difficulty walking, sex, age
Lifestyle Features: physical activity, fruit consumption, vegetable consumption, heavy alcohol consumption, any healthcare access, no access to healthcare due to cost
Socioeconomic Features: income & education
